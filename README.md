      </div>
      <div class="reason-card">
        <span class="reason-card__num">03</span>

        <h3>Integrates with your existing infra</h3>
        <p>Undeflected emails route to your Slack channels or email DLs. Voice and chat channels stay available for customers who need high-touch support.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="reason-card">
        <span class="reason-card__num">06</span>
        <h3>Day-zero compliance</h3>
        <p>Start with compliant logs from the first email. Every interaction is recorded and audit-ready for potential future reference. Out of the box, no setup.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="reason-card">
        <span class="reason-card__num">07</span>
        <h3>Auto-generated knowledge content</h3>
        <p>Successful AI responses become draft knowledge articles — ready for human review. Your support library grows automatically as the agent resolves more queries.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="reason-card">
        <span class="reason-card__num">08</span>
        <h3>Self-serve setup in 5 minutes</h3>
        <p>Register your support email, connect your Slack or DL, and the agent is live. No IT admin, no implementation project, no vendor calls.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="reason-card">
        <span class="reason-card__num">09</span>
        <h3>Smart routing to subject matter experts</h3>
        <p>Undeflected queries auto-assign to the right people. Over time, the agent learns which topics go to which Slack channel or team member.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="reason-card">
        <span class="reason-card__num">10</span>
